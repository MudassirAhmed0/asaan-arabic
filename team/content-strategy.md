# Instagram Content Strategy

## Core Principles (from GPT review)

1. **Copy first, design second.** If the copy is weak, no design saves it.
2. **Max ~6 words per line.** One idea per slide.
3. **Emotional arc, not information dump.** IG users follow feeling, not logic.
4. **Don't introduce the app before slide 4.** Earn attention first.
5. **If a slide feels "safe" or "polite", it's wrong.**
6. **Design serves the sentence, not decorates it.**
7. **If it can be understood zoomed out to 10%, it passes.**
