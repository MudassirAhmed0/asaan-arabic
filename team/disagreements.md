# Open Disagreements

Unresolved debates where team members have opposing views. These stay here until the CEO makes a call.

## Format

```
### [DISAGREE-XXX] Title
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Positions:**
- [Role A]: [Their position and reasoning]
- [Role B]: [Their position and reasoning]
**Impact if unresolved:** [What happens if we don't decide]
**CEO Status:** Pending review / Scheduled for standup
```
